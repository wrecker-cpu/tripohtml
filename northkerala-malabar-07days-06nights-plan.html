7 Days | 6 Nights - 2N Kannur - 2N Coorg - 2N Wayanad

Day 1 : KANNUR ARRIVAL. SIGHTSEEING

Kannur, widely known as the land of Looms and Lores is also the land of Theyyam. This magnificent art form plays an integral part in the culture of this Northern Kerala district. Kannur is a home to many unspoilt beaches, scenic hills, historic forts, captivating ritual arts and delectable cuisine. , the Kannur district in North Kerala is an ideal destination for an experiential getaway. On arrival in Kannur international Airport(CNN)/Railway Station (CAN) in the morning, our representative will pick up and proceed for a city heritage tour of Kannur covering St Angelos Fort, Folklore Museum, Handloom weaving Centre, Parassinikadavu Muthappan Temple, Snake Park and evening Sunset House Boat Cruise for 2 hours at Valapatnam River with Tea snacks. Check-in to hotel. Overnight Stay in Kannur Hotel.

Day 2 : THALASSERY HERITAGE TOUR

After breakfast, proceed for Thalasseri Heritage Tour covering Muzhappilangadu Drive in Beach (Asiaâ€™s longest drive-in beach), Dharmadam Island (You can walk to the island from Dharmadam Beach during low tide) Thalassery Pier, Overburryâ€™s Folly (Seaside park with a watch tower), Gundert Bangalow (Where Dr. Hermann Gundert lived before turning into a museum. It was constructed in the 1800â€™s), Enjoy Famous Thalassery Biriyani. Sree Perlassery Temple and Stepwell (National Water heritage site) etc. and evening drive back to visit Payyambalan Beach. Back to hotel. Overnight stay in Kannur Hotel.

Day 3 : KANNUR-COORG (2.5 hrs)

DAY 03 | KANNUR-COORG (2.5 hrs) After Breakfast, Check-out and drive to Coorg (2.5 hrs). Enjoy the drive through the Kerala-Karnataka forest. On arrival in Coorg, visit Madikeri Fort (also called Mercara Fort built by Mudduraja in the 17th century), Omkareshwara Temple (an ancient shrine dedicated to Lord Shiva) and Raja Seat (Popular lore claims that Kodava kings spent their evenings here. It is built on an elevated ground overseeing the valleys that lie to the west, making it a popular viewpoint to watch sunrise and sunset). Check in to the resort. Rest of the day at leisure. Overnight Stay in Coorg hotel.

Day 4 : COORG LOCAL SIGHTSEEING

After early breakfast, Proceed to visit Thalakaveri (stands at a height of 1,276 meters above sea level is primarly a temple but the place where the cauvery river originates is well preserved)and Abbey Falls (at over 21 metres high, the Abbey Falls are tucked away amidst coffee and spice plantations of the Western Ghats). Back to the hotel. Overnight stay in Coorg hotel.

Day 5 : COORG-WAYANAD

After breakfast, check-out and drive to (Kushal Nagar, visit ) Golden Temple (One of the largest Tibetan settlements in India), Nisargadama Bird Park, Dubbare Elephant camp (It provides an Elephant interaction along with jungle safari, Feeding, bathing and some adventure activities like river rafting and drive to Madikeri and drive to Wayanad.) On arrival check in to hotel. Rest of the day at leisure. Overnight stay Wayanad hotel.

Day 6 : WAYANAD LOCAL

After breakfast, proceed to visit Banasura Earth Dam (Largest earth dam in India and the second largest in Asia), Meenmutty Water falls, Karlad lake, Karaouzha dam and Adventure park (you can enjoy many adventure activities there). Back to Hotel. Over night Stay in Wayanad Hotel.

Day 7 : WAYANAD - CALICUT DEPARTURE

After breakfast, Check out and Transfer to Calicut. Enroute visit Pookottu Lake, En-ooru Tribal Heritage village, Lakkidi View Point and Drop at Calicut airport/ Station as per your onward journey timing. tour Ends.
