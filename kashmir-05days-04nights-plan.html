After Breakfast, Proceed Towards Gulmarg. En Route, You Get To See The Beautiful Tangmarg Town And Drive Ahead On A Scenic Drive Of 14 Kilometers To Gulmarg. Arrive In Gulmarg Early In The Afternoon And Begin A Short Tour, Boarding The Gondola Cable Car System (The 08 Minutes Ropeway). Descend Back to Gulmarg After An Hour and Later Indulge In Some Horse Riding. Later, Return Back to Srinagar.

Overnight stay at Srinagar.

Overnight stay at Srinagar.

Overnight stay at Srinagar Houseboat.
