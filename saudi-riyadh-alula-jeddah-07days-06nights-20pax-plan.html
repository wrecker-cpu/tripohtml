Here’s Your Itinerary : Special itinerary in favor of Riyadh, Al Ula and Jeddah

7 Days 6 Nights | 20 PAX

Day 1 : Arrival in Riyadh

- Meet at Riyadh Airport..

- Visit Al Murabba Palace.

- Heading to have a traditional Lunch..

- Transfer to Hotel for Check- In.

- Enjoy visiting Masmak fortress & Al Dira Souk ( Old bazaar ).

- Drive to Kind Abdulaziz historical center and visit the National Museum.

- Transfer to Riyadh hotel accommodation and overnight.

Day 2 : Riyadh

- After Breakfast, Meet at Riyadh Hotel accommodation.

- Drive to visit Ad Diriyah Heritage City & Bujiri Park.

- Heading to have a traditional Lunch.

- Visit Al Rajhi Mosque.

- Drive to Kingdom Tower to enjoy the view.

- Drive back to the hotel accommodation and overnight.

Day 3 : Riyadh- Al Ula

- Morning Breakfast at Riyadh Hotel accommodation & Check- Out.

- Transfer to Riyadh Airport & Departure to Al Ula.

- Time to have a traditional Lunch.

- Transfer to hotel for Check- In.

- Visit Old Town Market.

- Experience the Oasis Heritage trail to the old town.

- Drive to Al Ula Hotel accommodation and Overnight.

Day 4 : Riyadh- Al Ula

Morning Breakfast at the Hotel.

Visit Madain Saleh ( Hegra Tour ).

Time to have a traditional Lunch.

Visit Jabal Abu Oud inscriptions & Wadi ostrich with a hike experience.

Move to Drive to Hurt Al Ouird to see the amazing view of Al Ula city from the top of the highest mountain.

Drive to Al Ula Hotel accommodation & Overnight.

Day 5 : Al Ula - Jeddah

Morning Breakfast at Al Ula hotel.

Visit ( Dadan, Jabal Ikmah ) Tour.

Drive back to the hotel for Check- Out.

Time to have lunch at a private farm.

Move to visit Natural Rock formations ( Elephant Rock )

Transfer to Al Ula airport & fly to Jeddah.

Arrive in Jeddah & transfer to the hotel.

Drive back to the hotel accommodation and overnight.

Day 6 : Al Ula - Jeddah

Breakfast at Jeddah Hotel. Meet afternoon.

Move to the historical area tour including Nassef’s house.

Discover the Al alawi traditional market which is the historical area.

Time to have traditional lunch.

Moveto theJeddahmuseum,Floating Mosque, Jeddah Sign, Visit Jeddah Fountain.

Head to Altayybat City ( Abdulraouf Khalil Museum)

Drive back to the hotel accommodation and overnight.

Day 7 : Jeddah

Breakfast at Jeddah Hotel.

Drive to Jeddah Flagpole ( Take some photos).

Drive to Jeddah Tower. ( Take some photos ).

Free time for shopping before departure.

Drive back to Jeddah hotel for Check- Out.

Time to have traditional Lunch.

Transfer to Jeddah Airport.
