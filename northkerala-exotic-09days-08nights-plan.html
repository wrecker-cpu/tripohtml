Here’s Your Itinerary : Special itinerary in favor of Exotic Kerala

9 Days | 8 Nights - 2N Kannur - 1N Thekkady - 1N Allepey - 1N Varkala - 2N Kovalam

Day 1 : COCHIN-MUNNAR (3hrs)

On arrival in Cochin, our representative will pick you up and proceed to Munnar. Visit Cheeyappara and Valara Waterfalls, Tea Plantations, and Spice Garden on the way. Reach Munnar and Check in to Hotel. Rest of the day at leisure. Overnight stay in Munnar.

Day 2 : MUNNAR

After breakfast, suggest to start the tour by 9 am covering Eravikulam National park visit followed by Tea Museum. Post lunch, you will visit the Mattuppetty Dam, Echo point, Kundala Lake, Viewpoints, Pothamedu view point and Botanical Garden etc. Back to Hotel Overnight Stay in Munnar.

Day 3 : MUNNAR-THEKKADY (3-4 hrs)

After Breakfast, Check-out and drive to Thekkady (Suggest to start by 9am as Munnar to Thekkady will backtake 3-4 hours.). Thekkady has one of the world's most fascinating natural wildlife reserves noted for its geography, diverse wildlife and scenic beauty. drive through panoramic western ghat hills covered with spice plantations and occasional view of waterfalls and streams. Enroute you can visit Spice Garden having plantations of spices like Cardamon, cloves, javitri etc. On reaching Thekkady, you may go for Periyar boating to see the Periyar wild life sanctuary (Tickets can be booked in advance or at the counter). In the evening, you can go for optional activities like Kathakali, Elephant ride, Elephant bath, and Ayurveda massages. (All the activities have separate entrance fees.). Overnight Stay in Thekkady.

Day 4 : THEKKADY-ALLEPPEY (3-4 hrs)

After early breakfast, Check out and drive to Alleppey/Kumarakom (Suggest t o start by 8.30 am) by 12 noon check-into the House boat for a day Cruise. It is the best way to explore the beauty of backwaters. Sit back and relax while the boat cruises through the narrow serene backwater canals. In this world of simple pleasures, you’ll skim past ancient Chinese fishing nets; water Lillie’s, lush paddy fields, coir villages, rustic homes, temples and coconut groves. Innumerable lagoons, lakes, canals, estuaries and Kuttanad paddy fields. Welcome Drink, Lunch, Evening Tea with snacks, dinner will be served on board and enjoy your evening. Overnight stay in the House Boat.

Day 5 : ALLEPPEY- VARKALA -3-3.5 hrs

After breakfast disembark from the houseboat and Transfer to varkala. Evening Spend the time at Varkala Beach. Overnight Stayin Varkala.

Day 6 : VARKALA

After breakfast, enjoy the day at leisure at Varkala Beach. Overnight stay in Varkala

Day 7 : VARKALA - KOVALAM

After breakfast, check out and drive to Kovalam. Rest of the day at leisure. Spend the evening at Kovalam Beach. Overnight Stat in Kovalam.

Day 8 : TRIVANDRUM CITY TOUR

After breakfast, proceed for a full day sightseeing tour of Trivandrum, Covering Padmanabha Temple, Nappier Museum, Vax Museum, Shopping at Local market etc. Overnight Stay.

Day 9 : TRIVANDRUM DEPARTURE

After breakfast, check out and drive to Trivandrum Airport for the Return journey back home. Service Ends.
